Democractic Erosion

This chart shows the change in Electoral Democracy over time. This index is a measure of polyarchy, literally “rule of many,” or the response of the government to the needs of its citizens and their role as political equals. A higher score indicates greater polyarchy, while a lower score indicates greater autocracy.
